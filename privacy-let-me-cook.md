#### Last updated: 2026


Let Me Cook! respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect

The app may use third-party services that collect limited technical data, including:
* Firebase (authentication and app functionality)
* Google AdMob (to display non-personalized advertisements)


These services may collect:
* Device information
* App usage data
* Anonymous identifiers

No personally identifiable information is sold or shared by Let Me Cook!.

## Advertising

Let Me Cook! displays non-personalized ads only.

Non-personalized ads:
* Do not use tracking across apps or websites
* Do not require advertising consent
* Are based only on general context (such as app content or region)

## Children’s Privacy

Let Me Cook! is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
